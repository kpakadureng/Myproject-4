Women's Bantamweight Division - Fighters

Julianna Pena

Age: 34

Record: 12-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Sikjitsu

Head Trainer: Rick Little

Julianna "The Venezuelan Vixen" Pena is a former UFC Women’s Bantamweight Champion, known for her toughness and relentless fighting style. Pena shocked the world with her victory over Amanda Nunes, showcasing her exceptional grappling and unyielding determination. Training at Sikjitsu under Rick Little, she has refined her striking while maintaining a strong grappling base. Pena’s career is defined by her resilience and ability to rise to the occasion against top-tier opponents. Outside the cage, she is a vocal advocate for women in sports and an inspiration to aspiring fighters everywhere.

Holly Holm

Age: 42

Record: 14-6 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Jackson Wink MMA

Head Trainer: Greg Jackson

Holly "The Preacher’s Daughter" Holm is a former UFC Women’s Bantamweight Champion and one of the most decorated strikers in MMA history. With a background as a professional boxer and kickboxer, Holm made a seamless transition to MMA under the guidance of Greg Jackson at Jackson Wink MMA. Her iconic head-kick knockout victory over Ronda Rousey remains one of the most memorable moments in UFC history. Holm is known for her exceptional footwork, discipline, and ability to execute game plans with precision. Beyond fighting, she is admired for her humility and commitment to uplifting her community.

Ketlen Vieira

Age: 32

Record: 13-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Nova Uniao

Head Trainer: Andre Pederneiras

Ketlen "Fenomeno" Vieira is a skilled Brazilian fighter known for her grappling and striking abilities. Training at Nova Uniao under Andre Pederneiras, Vieira has risen through the ranks with a series of impressive performances against top contenders. Her balanced approach to fighting, combining powerful strikes and effective submissions, has made her a dangerous threat in the division. Vieira’s dedication to improvement and her strong work ethic continue to make her a standout competitor.

Irene Aldana

Age: 35

Record: 14-7 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Mexican

Camp: Lobo Gym

Head Trainer: Francisco Grasso

Irene Aldana is a powerful striker and one of Mexico’s most prominent MMA fighters. Training at Lobo Gym under Francisco Grasso, Aldana has developed a dynamic striking arsenal and a knack for delivering exciting finishes. Her technical boxing skills, combined with a relentless fighting spirit, have earned her a place among the elite in the women’s bantamweight division. Aldana’s success has also played a pivotal role in inspiring the next generation of Mexican athletes to pursue MMA.

Raquel Pennington

Age: 35

Record: 15-8 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Triple Threat Gym

Head Trainer: Jason Martinez

Raquel "Rocky" Pennington is a durable and experienced fighter known for her grit and well-rounded skills. Training at Triple Threat Gym, Pennington has consistently competed against the best in the division, earning victories through determination and tactical execution. Her ability to adapt to different opponents and deliver hard-fought wins makes her a respected name in the sport. Outside the octagon, Pennington is a vocal advocate for mental health and equality, using her platform to make a positive impact.
