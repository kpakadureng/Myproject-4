Women's Featherweight Division - Fighters

Amanda Nunes

Age: 35

Record: 23-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: American Top Team

Head Trainer: Mike Brown

Amanda "The Lioness" Nunes is a two-division champion and widely regarded as the greatest female fighter of all time. Training at American Top Team, her dominance in striking and grappling has made her an icon in the sport.

Cris Cyborg

Age: 38

Record: 26-2-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Chute Boxe Academy

Head Trainer: Rudimar Fedrigo

Cris Cyborg is a legendary featherweight fighter known for her devastating power and relentless pace. With titles in multiple organizations, she remains a dominant force in the division, showcasing her striking and durability.

Felicia Spencer

Age: 32

Record: 9-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Canadian

Camp: Jungle MMA

Head Trainer: Mike Lee

Felicia Spencer is a grappling specialist known for her toughness and ability to endure adversity. Training at Jungle MMA, Spencer has proven to be a resilient and skilled competitor in the featherweight division.

Holly Holm

Age: 42

Record: 14-6 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Jackson Wink MMA

Head Trainer: Greg Jackson

Holly "The Preacher's Daughter" Holm is a former bantamweight champion and decorated striker. Known for her head-kick KO of Ronda Rousey, Holm continues to compete as one of the most respected fighters in the division.

Megan Anderson

Age: 33

Record: 11-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Australian

Camp: Glory MMA

Head Trainer: James Krause

Megan Anderson is a towering featherweight with exceptional reach and knockout power. Training at Glory MMA, Anderson's striking prowess has made her a notable competitor in the division.

Norma Dumont

Age: 33

Record: 9-2 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Nova Uniao

Head Trainer: Andre Pederneiras

Norma Dumont is a rising featherweight with a well-rounded skill set. Training under Andre Pederneiras, Dumont combines strong grappling and striking to establish herself as a formidable fighter.

Zarah Fairn

Age: 37

Record: 6-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: French

Camp: MMA Factory

Head Trainer: Fernand Lopez

Zarah "Infinite" Fairn is a powerful striker from France known for her aggressive stand-up game. Training at MMA Factory, Fairn has faced top-tier competition in the featherweight division.

Leah McCourt

Age: 31

Record: 7-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Northern Irish

Camp: SBG Ireland

Head Trainer: John Kavanagh

Leah McCourt is an emerging talent in the featherweight division known for her grappling and determination. Training at SBG Ireland, McCourt continues to develop as a well-rounded competitor under the guidance of John Kavanagh.
