Bantamweight Division - Fighters

Sean O'Malley

Age: 30

Record: 18-1-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: American

Camp: MMA Lab

Head Trainer: John Crouch

Sean "Sugar" O'Malley is the current UFC Bantamweight Champion, renowned for his striking precision and flamboyant style. Training out of MMA Lab under John Crouch, O'Malley has developed a unique fighting approach that combines unorthodox movements with knockout power. His charisma and social media presence have made him a fan favorite, while his performances in the octagon showcase his technical prowess and entertainment value.

Aljamain Sterling

Age: 35

Record: 24-4 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Serra-Longo Fight Team

Head Trainer: Ray Longo

Aljamain "Funk Master" Sterling is a former UFC Bantamweight Champion known for his grappling expertise and dynamic striking. Training under Ray Longo at Serra-Longo Fight Team, Sterling has showcased his ability to control opponents on the ground and execute submissions. His unorthodox style and athleticism have made him a standout in the division, earning victories over top-tier opponents.

Petr Yan

Age: 31

Record: 17-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Russian

Camp: Tiger Muay Thai

Head Trainer: John Hutchinson

Petr "No Mercy" Yan is a former UFC Bantamweight Champion recognized for his relentless pressure and boxing skills. Training out of Tiger Muay Thai, Yan has developed a well-rounded game that includes precise striking and solid takedown defense. His aggressive style and durability have made him a formidable opponent in the bantamweight division.

Cory Sandhagen

Age: 32

Record: 17-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Elevation Fight Team

Head Trainer: Christian Allen

Cory "The Sandman" Sandhagen is known for his fluid striking and versatility inside the octagon. Training with Elevation Fight Team under Christian Allen, Sandhagen utilizes his reach and movement to outstrike opponents. His creative techniques and adaptability have earned him highlight-reel finishes and a reputation as one of the most exciting fighters in the division.

Merab Dvalishvili

Age: 33

Record: 18-4 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Georgian

Camp: Serra-Longo Fight Team

Head Trainer: Ray Longo

Merab "The Machine" Dvalishvili is celebrated for his relentless pace and exceptional wrestling. Training alongside Aljamain Sterling at Serra-Longo Fight Team, Dvalishvili's cardio and takedown ability allow him to overwhelm opponents. His tenacity and work ethic have propelled him up the bantamweight rankings, making him a tough matchup for anyone in the division.
