Middleweight Division - Fighters

Israel Adesanya

Age: 34

Record: 24-2 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Nigerian-New Zealander

Camp: City Kickboxing

Head Trainer: Eugene Bareman

Israel "The Last Stylebender" Adesanya is a former UFC Middleweight Champion known for his striking mastery and fight IQ. Training at City Kickboxing, Adesanya's ability to control range and outmaneuver opponents has made him one of the greatest middleweights in MMA history.

Alex Pereira

Age: 36

Record: 7-2 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Teixeira MMA & Fitness

Head Trainer: Glover Teixeira

Alex "Poatan" Pereira is a former UFC Middleweight Champion and elite striker. Known for his devastating power and precision, Pereira has transitioned seamlessly from kickboxing to MMA, delivering memorable knockouts in the process.

Robert Whittaker

Age: 33

Record: 24-7 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Australian

Camp: Gracie Jiu-Jitsu Smeaton Grange

Head Trainer: Fabricio Itte

Robert "The Reaper" Whittaker is a former UFC Middleweight Champion known for his well-rounded game and resilience. His technical striking and grappling skills have made him a consistent top contender in the division.

Marvin "The Italian Dream" Vettori is a durable and determined fighter known for his relentless pace. Training at Kings MMA, Vettori combines striking and grappling to consistently challenge the division's elite.
