Light Heavyweight Division - Fighters

Jamahal Hill

Age: 32

Record: 12-1-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: American

Camp: Black Lion Jiu-Jitsu

Head Trainer: Chad Pomeroy

Jamahal "Sweet Dreams" Hill is the reigning UFC Light Heavyweight Champion and a rising star in the division. Known for his precise striking and powerful knockout ability, Hill's journey to the title has been one of perseverance and growth. Training at Black Lion Jiu-Jitsu, Hill has honed his skills to become a complete fighter, capable of dominating opponents both standing and on the ground. His head coach, Chad Pomeroy, has been instrumental in crafting Hill's strategic approach, making him a formidable presence in the octagon. With impressive wins over top contenders, Hill has quickly established himself as a fighter to watch in the light heavyweight division. His humility and work ethic outside the cage have also earned him the respect of fans and peers alike.
