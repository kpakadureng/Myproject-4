Welterweight Division - Fighters

Leon Edwards

Age: 32

Record: 21-3-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: British

Camp: Team Renegade

Head Trainer: Dave Lovell

Leon "Rocky" Edwards is the reigning UFC Welterweight Champion, known for his well-rounded skills and resilience. Training at Team Renegade, Edwards has risen to prominence through consistency and a memorable head-kick KO against Kamaru Usman.

Kamaru Usman

Age: 36

Record: 20-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Nigerian-American

Camp: ONX Sports

Head Trainer: Trevor Wittman

Kamaru "The Nigerian Nightmare" Usman is a former UFC Welterweight Champion with a dominant wrestling base and improved striking. Known for his incredible cardio and mental toughness, Usman remains one of the top welterweights in the world.

Colby Covington

Age: 35

Record: 17-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: MMA Masters

Head Trainer: Cesar Carneiro

Colby "Chaos" Covington is known for his relentless pace and wrestling. Training at MMA Masters, Covington has consistently showcased elite cardio and striking pressure, making him a perennial title contender.

Gilbert Burns

Age: 37

Record: 22-6 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Kill Cliff FC

Head Trainer: Henri Hooft

Gilbert "Durinho" Burns is a grappling ace with knockout power. Training under Henri Hooft, Burns has proven his versatility and heart, earning wins against top-tier welterweights.

Belal Muhammad

Age: 35

Record: 23-3-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Palestinian-American

Camp: Roufusport

Head Trainer: Duke Roufus

Belal "Remember the Name" Muhammad is a well-rounded fighter known for his cardio and tactical striking. Training at Roufusport, Muhammad has worked his way into title contention with consistent performances.

Stephen Thompson

Age: 40

Record: 17-6-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: American

Camp: Upstate Karate

Head Trainer: Ray Thompson

Stephen "Wonderboy" Thompson is a karate specialist known for his striking precision and sportsmanship. With a unique style, Thompson remains a fan favorite and a threat to any opponent in the welterweight division.

Jorge Masvidal

Age: 38

Record: 35-17 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: American Top Team

Head Trainer: Mike Brown

Jorge "Gamebred" Masvidal is a veteran known for his striking, resilience, and memorable knockouts. Training at American Top Team, Masvidal's career resurgence has been marked by iconic moments like the fastest KO in UFC history.

Shavkat Rakhmonov

Age: 29

Record: 17-0 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Kazakh

Camp: Sanford MMA

Head Trainer: Henri Hooft

Shavkat "Nomad" Rakhmonov is an undefeated prospect with a perfect finishing rate. Known for his balanced skills and composure, Rakhmonov is one of the brightest rising stars in the welterweight division.

Michael Chiesa

Age: 36

Record: 18-6 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Sikjitsu

Head Trainer: Rick Little

Michael "Maverick" Chiesa is a grappling specialist known for his strength and submissions. Training at Sikjitsu, Chiesa's size and wrestling make him a challenging matchup for any welterweight.
