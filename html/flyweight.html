Flyweight Division - Fighters

Alexandre Pantoja

Age: 34

Record: 29-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: American Top Team

Head Trainer: Marcus "Buchecha" Almeida

Alexandre Pantoja is the reigning UFC Flyweight Champion and one of the most well-rounded athletes in the division. Fighting out of American Top Team, he is known for his incredible grappling, calculated striking, and unrelenting pressure. Under the guidance of head trainer Marcus "Buchecha" Almeida, Pantoja has continuously evolved his skill set. His resilience and ability to adapt in high-stakes fights make him a dangerous contender for anyone in the division.

Brandon Moreno

Age: 30

Record: 21-7-2 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Mexican

Camp: Entram Gym

Head Trainer: Raul Arvizu

Brandon Moreno, nicknamed "The Assassin Baby," is a former UFC Flyweight Champion and a fan favorite. Known for his grit and determination, Moreno fights out of Entram Gym in Tijuana, Mexico, under the tutelage of Raul Arvizu. His exciting fighting style combines sharp boxing, scrambles, and an iron will, making him one of the most beloved fighters in the division. His journey from near-release from the UFC to becoming champion is an inspirational story of perseverance.

Kai Kara-France

Age: 31

Record: 24-11 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: New Zealander

Camp: City Kickboxing

Head Trainer: Eugene Bareman

Kai Kara-France is a dynamic striker fighting out of the world-renowned City Kickboxing gym in Auckland, New Zealand. Trained by Eugene Bareman, Kara-France has developed a sharp and technical striking arsenal complemented by a durable ground game. Known for his knockout power and crowd-pleasing style, he has cemented himself as a top contender in the flyweight division. Kara-France’s resilience and determination make him a fighter to watch in every bout.

Askar Askarov

Age: 31

Record: 14-1-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Russian

Camp: Akhmat Fight Club

Head Trainer: Abdusalam Ulyanov

Askar Askarov is one of the most technically gifted grapplers in the flyweight division, with an impressive background in freestyle wrestling and sambo. Fighting out of Akhmat Fight Club in Grozny, Russia, Askarov is known for his relentless takedowns and suffocating top control. Under the mentorship of head trainer Abdusalam Ulyanov, he has polished his striking to complement his elite ground game. His ability to neutralize opponents makes him a formidable force in the division.

Matheus Nicolau

Age: 30

Record: 19-3-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Nova Uniao

Head Trainer: Andre Pederneiras

Matheus Nicolau is a rising star in the UFC Flyweight division, combining technical precision with calm composure. Brandon Royval

Manel Kape
