Super Lightweight Division - Fighters

Kevin Lee

Age: 31

Record: 18-7 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Sanford MMA

Head Trainer: Henri Hooft

Kevin "The Motown Phenom" Lee is a dynamic fighter known for his powerful wrestling and striking versatility. Training under Henri Hooft at Sanford MMA, Lee has excelled in grappling exchanges and developed a dangerous striking arsenal. His athleticism and fight IQ make him a top competitor in the super lightweight division.

Mike Perry

Age: 32

Record: 14-8 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Fusion X-Cel

Head Trainer: Julian Williams

Mike "Platinum" Perry is a hard-hitting striker known for his aggressive style and knockout power. Training at Fusion X-Cel under Julian Williams, Perry has showcased his resilience and ability to brawl with the best. His unorthodox techniques and fearless approach make him a thrilling fighter to watch.

Dan Hooker

Age: 34

Record: 22-12 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: New Zealander

Camp: City Kickboxing

Head Trainer: Eugene Bareman

Dan "The Hangman" Hooker is a versatile striker and grappler, known for his toughness and finishing ability. Training under Eugene Bareman at City Kickboxing, Hooker has developed a balanced style that keeps opponents guessing. His endurance and willingness to engage in wars have solidified his reputation in the super lightweight division.

Brad Riddell

Age: 32

Record: 10-4 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: New Zealander

Camp: City Kickboxing

Head Trainer: Eugene Bareman

Brad "Quake" Riddell is a technical striker with an impressive kickboxing background. Training at City Kickboxing under Eugene Bareman, Riddell has transitioned his striking expertise into a comprehensive MMA game. His precision and ability to adapt make him a strong contender in the super lightweight division.

Rafael dos Anjos

Age: 39

Record: 32-14 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Evolve MMA

Head Trainer: Chatri Sityodtong

Rafael dos Anjos is a former UFC Lightweight Champion known for his well-rounded skills and relentless pressure. Training at Evolve MMA, dos Anjos has developed a versatile game that includes striking, grappling, and a strong clinch game. His experience and durability make him a formidable opponent in any division.

Islam Makhachev

Age: 31

Record: 24-1 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Russian

Camp: American Kickboxing Academy

Head Trainer: Javier Mendez

Islam Makhachev is a dominant grappler and the protege of Khabib Nurmagomedov. Training under Javier Mendez at AKA, Makhachev has mastered the art of control and submissions. His striking improvements and elite wrestling have established him as a top fighter in the super lightweight division.

Arman Tsarukyan

Age: 26

Record: 19-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Armenian

Camp: American Top Team

Head Trainer: Ricardo Liborio

Arman Tsarukyan is a rising prospect with a well-rounded skill set and exceptional cardio. Training under Ricardo Liborio at ATT, Tsarukyan has showcased his ability to compete at the highest level with impressive grappling and striking. His potential and work ethic make him a future star in the division.

Mateusz Gamrot

Age: 32

Record: 22-2-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Polish

Camp: Czerwony Smok

Head Trainer: Andrzej Kościelski

Mateusz "Gamer" Gamrot is a former KSW Champion and a grappling specialist with an evolving striking game. Training under Andrzej Kościelski at Czerwony Smok, Gamrot has displayed relentless pressure and a high fight IQ. His ability to adapt in the octagon makes him a dangerous opponent in the super lightweight division.
