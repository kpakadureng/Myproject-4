Atomweight Division - Fighters

Angela Lee

Age: 27

Record: 11-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Canadian-American

Camp: United MMA

Head Trainer: Ken Lee

Angela "Unstoppable" Lee is a former ONE Atomweight Champion known for her grappling expertise and warrior spirit. Training under her father, Ken Lee, at United MMA, Angela has showcased a diverse skill set that includes submissions and striking. Her resilience and ability to adapt make her one of the top atomweight fighters in the world.

Seo Hee Ham

Age: 36

Record: 26-8 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: South Korean

Camp: Team MAD

Head Trainer: Yang Seung-Bae

Seo Hee Ham, nicknamed "Hamderlei Silva," is a seasoned veteran known for her relentless striking and durability. Training out of Team MAD in South Korea, Ham has faced some of the best competition in the atomweight division. Her aggressive style and technical prowess have earned her respect as a true competitor in the sport.

Alyse Anderson

Age: 28

Record: 6-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Michigan Top Team

Head Trainer: James Lee

Alyse "Lil' Savage" Anderson is a rising star in the atomweight division, known for her well-rounded skills and determination. Training at Michigan Top Team under James Lee, Anderson has shown great promise with her ability to adapt in both striking and grappling exchanges. Her focus and work ethic make her a fighter to watch.

Denice Zamboanga

Age: 27

Record: 9-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Filipino

Camp: Marrok Force

Head Trainer: George Hickman

Denice "The Menace" Zamboanga is a well-rounded fighter from the Philippines, known for her aggressive style and resilience. Training under George Hickman at Marrok Force, Zamboanga has honed her striking and grappling skills to compete against the best in the division. Her determination and passion make her a fierce competitor.
