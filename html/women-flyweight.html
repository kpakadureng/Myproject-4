Women's Flyweight Division - Fighters

Valentina Shevchenko

Age: 35

Record: 23-4 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Kyrgyzstani-Peruvian

Camp: Tiger Muay Thai

Head Trainer: Pavel Fedotov

Valentina "Bullet" Shevchenko is widely regarded as one of the greatest fighters in women’s MMA history. Known for her elite striking, impeccable defense, and strategic brilliance, Shevchenko held the UFC Flyweight title for several years, successfully defending it against top-tier opponents. Training under Pavel Fedotov, Valentina combines her Muay Thai background with world-class grappling, making her a complete fighter capable of excelling in every aspect of the sport. Her dominance and dedication to her craft have made her a role model for fighters worldwide.

Alexa Grasso

Age: 30

Record: 16-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Mexican

Camp: Lobo Gym

Head Trainer: Francisco Grasso

Alexa Grasso is the current UFC Flyweight Champion and a trailblazer for women’s MMA in Mexico. Known for her technical boxing, sharp footwork, and evolving ground game, Grasso captured the title with a stunning performance against Valentina Shevchenko. Training at Lobo Gym under the guidance of Francisco Grasso, Alexa has become one of the most well-rounded fighters in the division. Her journey from a promising prospect to a world champion has inspired countless young athletes in Mexico and beyond.

Taila Santos

Age: 30

Record: 19-2 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Astra Fight Team

Head Trainer: Marcelo Brigadeiro

Taila Santos is a powerful and well-rounded competitor in the UFC Flyweight Division. Training at Astra Fight Team under Marcelo Brigadeiro, Santos has showcased her ability to dominate fights with both her striking and grappling. Her near-victory against Valentina Shevchenko demonstrated her potential to compete with the very best. Taila’s work ethic and determination have made her a standout contender, and she continues to be a serious threat to the division’s elite.

Manon Fiorot

Age: 33

Record: 10-1 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: French

Camp: Boxing Squad

Head Trainer: Aldric Cassata

Manon "The Beast" Fiorot is a rising star in the UFC Flyweight Division. Known for her crisp striking and relentless pace, Fiorot has steadily climbed the rankings with dominant performances. Training at Boxing Squad under Aldric Cassata, she has developed a striking-heavy style that keeps opponents on the defensive. Fiorot’s dedication to her craft and her aspirations of becoming a world champion make her one of the most exciting fighters to watch in the division.

Casey O’Neill

Age: 26

Record: 9-1 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Scottish-Australian

Camp: Xtreme Couture

Head Trainer: Eric Nicksick

Casey "King" O’Neill is a young and ambitious fighter making waves in the flyweight division. Known for her relentless pace and aggressive grappling, O’Neill has quickly become a fan favorite. Training at Xtreme Couture under Eric Nicksick, she has honed her skills to take on the best in the division. Her energy and determination to climb the ranks ensure she is a future title contender in the making.
