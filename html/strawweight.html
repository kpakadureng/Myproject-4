Strawweight Division - Fighters

Rose Namajunas

Age: 31

Record: 11-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: 303 Training Center

Head Trainer: Trevor Wittman

Rose "Thug" Namajunas is a two-time UFC Strawweight Champion and one of the most beloved fighters in mixed martial arts. Known for her grace inside the cage and her relentless fighting spirit, Rose’s journey to the top has been nothing short of inspirational. Growing up in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Rose faced significant challenges in her early life that shaped her resilience and determination. Her martial arts journey began with taekwondo, followed by karate and Brazilian jiu-jitsu, disciplines that formed the foundation of her dynamic fighting style.

Namajunas burst onto the scene as a contestant on The Ultimate Fighter, where her charisma and skills caught the attention of fans worldwide. Her defining moment came in 2017, when she shocked the world with a first-round knockout of Joanna Jedrzejczyk to capture the strawweight title. Rose’s ability to combine technical precision with a fearless mentality makes her a unique competitor. Whether it’s her fluid striking or her dangerous submission game, she always finds a way to adapt and overcome.

Outside the cage, Rose is an advocate for mental health awareness and a role model for resilience and perseverance. Her close bond with her fiancé and former UFC fighter Pat Barry serves as a source of strength and inspiration. As she continues to cement her legacy, Rose Namajunas remains a beacon of hope and excellence, proving that champions are forged not just through victories but through the battles they overcome both inside and outside the octagon.

Zhang Weili

Age: 34

Record: 23-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Chinese

Camp: Bangtao Muay Thai & MMA

Head Trainer: George Hickman

Zhang "Magnum" Weili is a trailblazer for MMA in China and the first Chinese fighter to capture a UFC title. Her journey to becoming a world champion is a testament to her unyielding work ethic and love for the sport. Growing up in a small town in Hebei Province, Zhang initially worked as a fitness instructor before pursuing a professional MMA career. Her relentless drive and raw talent quickly propelled her to prominence.

Weili’s historic victory over Jessica Andrade in 2019 made her a national hero, solidifying her status as one of the best strawweights in the world. Known for her explosive striking, impeccable conditioning, and well-rounded skill set, Zhang has delivered some of the most memorable fights in recent history. Her epic battle with Joanna Jedrzejczyk at UFC 248, widely regarded as one of the greatest MMA fights of all time, showcased her resilience and championship mettle.

Training at Bangtao Muay Thai & MMA under George Hickman, Zhang continues to evolve as a fighter, incorporating new techniques and strategies to stay ahead of the competition. Her dedication to inspiring the next generation of athletes in China and her humble, hardworking demeanor have made her a global ambassador for the sport.

Carla Esparza

Age: 36

Record: 19-7 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Team Oyama

Head Trainer: Colin Oyama

Carla "Cookie Monster" Esparza is the inaugural UFC Strawweight Champion and a pioneer in the division. Known for her dominant wrestling and grappling, Esparza paved the way for the development of the strawweight class with her historic victory in The Ultimate Fighter finale. Training under Colin Oyama, Carla’s tactical approach and relentless pressure have made her a perennial contender throughout her career.

Esparza’s career highlights include reclaiming the strawweight title with a split-decision victory over Rose Namajunas in 2022, showcasing her ability to adapt and persevere. Beyond her grappling pedigree, Carla has worked tirelessly to evolve her striking, making her a more well-rounded threat in the octagon. Her dedication to improvement and her willingness to take on tough opponents have earned her the respect of fans and fighters alike.

Outside the cage, Carla is known for her friendly demeanor and her passion for culinary arts, often sharing her cooking adventures with fans on social media. As one of the division’s founding figures, Esparza’s impact on women’s MMA continues to inspire the next generation of fighters to chase their dreams and make their mark in the sport.

Jessica Andrade

Age: 32

Record: 24-12 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Parana Vale Tudo

Head Trainer: Gilliard Parana

Jessica "Bate Estaca" Andrade is a former UFC Strawweight Champion known for her ferocious power and aggressive style. Training at Parana Vale Tudo under Gilliard Parana, Andrade has earned a reputation as one of the most exciting fighters in MMA. Her slam KO victory over Rose Namajunas to capture the title in 2019 remains one of the most iconic moments in the sport.

Andrade’s fighting style is defined by her relentless pressure and ability to impose her will on opponents. Whether it’s her heavy hands, devastating slams, or underrated submission game, Jessica always brings an unpredictable and thrilling element to her fights. Her willingness to compete across multiple weight classes further showcases her fearless approach to competition.

Beyond her accomplishments in the cage, Jessica is celebrated for her humility and connection with fans. As one of Brazil’s most accomplished female fighters, she serves as a role model for young athletes aspiring to reach the highest levels of the sport. Andrade’s dedication to excellence and her never-back-down attitude ensure her place among the elite in women’s MMA.

Joanna Jedrzejczyk

Age: 35

Record: 16-5 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Polish

Camp: American Top Team

Head Trainer: Mike Brown

Joanna Jedrzejczyk is a former UFC Strawweight Champion and one of the most dominant fighters in the history of the division. Known for her pinpoint striking, exceptional cardio, and championship mentality, Joanna held the title from 2015 to 2017, defending it successfully five times. Training at American Top Team under Mike Brown, Joanna has developed a reputation for her technical prowess and relentless pace in the octagon.

Her intense battles with the division's top contenders, including her legendary fight against Zhang Weili at UFC 248, have cemented her legacy as a true warrior. Joanna's combination of Muay Thai and kickboxing skills has made her one of the most feared strikers in women’s MMA. Outside the cage, Joanna is known for her charisma, business ventures, and dedication to inspiring women to pursue their dreams.

Despite stepping away from active competition in recent years, Joanna's influence on the sport continues to be felt, and her name remains synonymous with excellence in the strawweight division.

Marina Rodriguez

Age: 36

Record: 16-2-2 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Thai Brasil

Head Trainer: Marcio Malko

Marina Rodriguez is a rising star in the UFC Strawweight Division, known for her technical striking and ability to adapt to any opponent. Training at Thai Brasil under Marcio Malko, Rodriguez has demonstrated a diverse skill set that combines sharp Muay Thai techniques with solid grappling defense. Her precision striking and counterattacks make her a challenging opponent for anyone in the division.

Rodriguez has steadily climbed the rankings with notable wins over former champions and top contenders. Her resilience and ability to maintain composure under pressure have made her a fan favorite. As she continues her ascent toward title contention, Rodriguez remains a fighter to watch in the competitive strawweight division.

Outside the cage, Marina is a strong advocate for empowering women through sports, using her platform to inspire the next generation of athletes in Brazil and beyond.

Mackenzie Dern

Age: 30

Record: 12-3 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American-Brazilian

Camp: RVCA Training Center

Head Trainer: Jason Parillo

Mackenzie Dern is a highly skilled grappler and one of the most accomplished submission artists in the UFC Strawweight Division. A Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt under her father, Wellington Dias, Dern has seamlessly transitioned her world-class grappling into MMA, securing multiple submission victories inside the octagon. Training under Jason Parillo at RVCA Training Center, Dern has also improved her striking, making her a more well-rounded competitor.

Her journey in MMA has been marked by determination and growth, as she continues to refine her skills while balancing her career and family life. Dern's dedication to her craft and her passion for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu make her a standout in the division and a role model for young fighters.

Off the mat, Mackenzie is known for her approachable personality and her commitment to raising awareness about the mental and physical demands of professional fighting.

Tatiana Suarez

Age: 33

Record: 9-0 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Millennia MMA

Head Trainer: Baret Yoshida

Tatiana Suarez is an undefeated powerhouse in the strawweight division, known for her dominant wrestling and ground control. A former Olympic wrestling hopeful, Suarez turned to MMA after overcoming a battle with cancer, channeling her competitive spirit and discipline into the sport. Training at Millennia MMA under Baret Yoshida, she has developed into a well-rounded fighter with relentless takedowns and a formidable submission game.

Suarez's inspirational journey has earned her widespread admiration from fans and peers alike. Her combination of technical excellence and mental toughness makes her one of the most feared competitors in the division. As she continues her undefeated streak, Suarez is poised to become a title contender and a role model for overcoming adversity.

Outside the cage, Tatiana advocates for cancer awareness and uses her story to inspire others to pursue their dreams, no matter the obstacles.
