Lightweight Division - Fighters

Islam Makhachev

Age: 31

Record: 24-1 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Russian

Camp: American Kickboxing Academy

Head Trainer: Javier Mendez

Islam Makhachev is the reigning UFC Lightweight Champion, known for his elite grappling and improved striking. Training under Javier Mendez at AKA, Makhachev has established himself as a dominant force in the division, carrying the legacy of his mentor, Khabib Nurmagomedov.

Charles Oliveira

Age: 34

Record: 34-9 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Brazilian

Camp: Chute Boxe Diego Lima

Head Trainer: Diego Lima

Charles "Do Bronx" Oliveira is a former UFC Lightweight Champion and the all-time leader in UFC finishes and submissions. Training at Chute Boxe Diego Lima, Oliveira combines creative grappling with explosive striking to remain one of the most dangerous fighters in the division.

Dustin Poirier

Age: 35

Record: 29-8 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: American Top Team

Head Trainer: Mike Brown

Dustin "The Diamond" Poirier is a former interim UFC Lightweight Champion known for his grit and technical striking. Training under Mike Brown at American Top Team, Poirier's well-rounded skills and warrior spirit have made him a fan favorite and perennial title contender.

Justin Gaethje

Age: 34

Record: 25-4 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Elevation Fight Team

Head Trainer: Trevor Wittman

Justin "The Highlight" Gaethje is a former interim UFC Lightweight Champion known for his knockout power and exciting fight style. Training under Trevor Wittman, Gaethje combines precision striking with relentless aggression, making him a must-watch fighter.

Beneil Dariush

Age: 34

Record: 22-5-1 (Wins-Losses-Draws)

Nationality: Iranian-American

Camp: Kings MMA

Head Trainer: Rafael Cordeiro

Beneil Dariush is a grappling specialist with an ever-evolving striking game. Training under Rafael Cordeiro at Kings MMA, Dariush's durability and skillset make him one of the most well-rounded fighters in the lightweight division.

Michael Chandler

Age: 37

Record: 23-8 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Sanford MMA

Head Trainer: Henri Hooft

Michael Chandler is a former Bellator Lightweight Champion known for his explosive power and wrestling. Training under Henri Hooft at Sanford MMA, Chandler brings intensity and heart to every fight, earning him multiple Fight of the Night awards in the UFC.
