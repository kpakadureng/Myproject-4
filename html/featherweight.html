Featherweight Division - Fighters

Alexander Volkanovski

Age: 35

Record: 26-2 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: Australian

Camp: Freestyle Fighting Gym

Head Trainer: Joe Lopez

Alexander "The Great" Volkanovski is the reigning UFC Featherweight Champion, known for his versatility, cardio, and adaptability in the octagon. Training under Joe Lopez at Freestyle Fighting Gym, Volkanovski has developed a unique skill set that blends striking and grappling seamlessly. His dominance in the division and wins over elite competitors have solidified his legacy as one of the greatest featherweights of all time.

Max Holloway

Age: 32

Record: 24-7 (Wins-Losses)

Nationality: American

Camp: Gracie Technics

Head Trainer: Rylan Lizares

Max "Blessed" Holloway is a former UFC Featherweight Champion, renowned for his high-volume striking and incredible durability. Training at Gracie Technics, Holloway's ability to overwhelm opponents with relentless pressure and precise combinations has made him a fan favorite. Known for his iconic battles with the division's elite, Holloway remains a top contender and an inspiration to aspiring fighters.
